frequently asked questions

Who can use the gym facilities?

The Mini Gym FT-UMJ facilities are available for all UMJ academic community members, including students, lecturers, and staff. Students from other faculties are also allowed to use these facilities.

How is the procedure to become a gym member?

The registration process to become a member of the Mini Gym FT-UMJ is as follows:
Confirmation to Gym Management Staff
Enter the Gym
Fill out the Attendance List

What are the rules and regulations that apply to this gym?

The rules and regulations for using the Mini Gym FT-UMJ include maintaining cleanliness and durability of gym equipment, regulating queues and equipment usage, maintaining cleanliness and comfort of the gym space, complying with gym capacity limits, and respecting the privacy of other users.

How is the separation of sessions between in this gym?

The Mini Gym FT-UMJ implements a system of separating sessions between males and females. This is done by dividing the gym space based on gender. Male and female members are not allowed to use the gym space simultaneously to maintain the privacy and comfort of users. Conducting separate sessions for males and females also aims to create a more comfortable and inclusive environment for all users.
